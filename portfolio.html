Cosmos

Cosmos is a beauty, tattoo, and wellness marketplace concept built as a polished front-end profile and booking experience. The project is designed to present service providers clearly, explain what they offer, and make it easier for clients to request appointments with the right professional.

Overview

Premium presentation for service-based professionals

This repository contains a static website prototype for Cosmos. It focuses on premium presentation, clear service communication, and a clean booking flow for professionals working across multiple service categories.

Stylist Services

Cosmos supports salon and independent hair professionals with detailed service presentation before booking.

  • Haircuts and precision cuts
  • Coloring and blonding services
  • Balayage and lived-in color
  • Extensions
  • Scalp and hair treatments
  • Bridal and event styling

Tattoo Artist Services

Artists can showcase their style, rates, studio details, portfolio links, and booking requirements.

  • Custom tattoo consultations
  • Small, medium, and large sessions
  • Fine line, realism, blackwork, geometric, and illustrative work
  • Cover-ups
  • Touch-ups

Massage And Wellness Services

Wellness providers can present service types, preferences, and notes that help prepare for appointments.

  • Swedish massage
  • Deep tissue massage
  • Hot stone therapy
  • Sports massage
  • Prenatal massage
  • Reflexology, lymphatic drainage, aromatherapy, and Thai massage

Appointment Booking

Clients can request appointments with the details providers need up front.

  • Contact information
  • Preferred date and time
  • Service type and service-specific notes
  • Professional preferences
  • Optional transportation details
Highlights

What this portfolio item demonstrates

Provider Profiles

Cosmos is built to highlight biographies, experience, service listings, pricing, availability, ratings, and showcase content so clients can compare providers more easily.

Booking Experience

The concept uses a structured intake flow to collect useful details early and make appointment confirmation more efficient for professionals.

Project Features

Static single-page site in index.html, premium editorial-style visual design, service cards with pricing and descriptions, multi-category booking form, and responsive structure for smaller screens.

Use Case

This works as a portfolio piece, a landing page concept, a design prototype, or a starting point for a larger marketplace booking platform.
